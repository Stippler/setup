general:
	- open terminal: mod+enter
	- open search: mod+d
	- end application: mod+shift+q

	- stack windows: mod+s
	- tile windows: mod+e
	- tab windows: mod+w
	- resize: mod+r
	- mod+shift+arrow: move window

	- move to different workspace: mod+number
	- move window to different workspace: mod+shift+number

stuff:
